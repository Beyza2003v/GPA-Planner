GPA Planner is an interactive academic planning tool that enables university students to analyze their past performance, understand their current academic standing, and simulate future outcomes under realistic university policies.

The application allows users to upload transcript PDFs, review and adjust grades from previously completed courses, match courses with curriculum and elective requirements, and dynamically calculate GPA based on both completed and planned coursework.

By supporting what-if scenarios across past, present, and future courses, GPA Planner provides a comprehensive and flexible approach to academic decision-making.
